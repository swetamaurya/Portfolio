Landing Page

A landing page is a focused, standalone web page designed with a specific purpose in mind, often related to marketing or lead generation. It's where users "land" after clicking on an advertisement, link, or search result. The primary goal of a landing page is to guide visitors toward a single call to action (CTA) or conversion objective. This could be signing up for a newsletter, downloading a resource, making a purchase, or any other action that aligns with the page's intended purpose.
